## Executive Summary

Research conducted across Google SERPs (Morocco), competitor analysis (mano.ma, allopro.ma, mesdepanneurs.co, m3allem.ma, sosdepanneur.com), and market signals. Volume estimates are based on:
- SERP competition density (number of dedicated pages/domains)
- Competitor presence and ad activity
- Morocco internet penetration (~35M users, ~88% mobile)
- 84% of Google Ads traffic in Morocco is mobile
- Seasonal patterns: peaks during Ramadan prep, September-December

**Key finding**: The Moroccan home services market is moderately competitive online. Most competitors are local single-service sites or directories. No dominant aggregator exists yet, creating a massive opportunity for allo-maison.ma.
